System Actors

Guest User

Anonymous users who can browse products and add to cart without registration

Registered Customer

Authenticated users who can purchase items, place orders, and track order history

Administrator

System managers who control product catalog, orders, and view analytics

Key Features by Actor

Guest Users Can:

  • Browse laptop catalog with pagination
  • Search and filter products
  • View detailed product information
  • Add items to shopping cart
  • Register for an account

Registered Customers Can (All of Above):

  • Place orders with shipping address
  • View complete order history
  • Track individual order details
  • Manage their profile
  • Logout and manage sessions

Administrators Can:

  • Create, read, update, delete products
  • Manage product inventory
  • View and manage all customer orders
  • Update order status (Pending → Processing → Shipped → Delivered)
  • View revenue reports and analytics
